`<cite>` 标签指引用某文献的名字
----

The case <cite>Hugo v. Danielle</cite> is relevant here.


`<dfn>` 表示被定义的词汇
----

The term <dfn>organic food</dfn> refers to food produced without synthetic chemicals.

<dfn>有机食物</dfn>这个词指的是无化学肥料培养出来的食品。
